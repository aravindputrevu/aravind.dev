It is a well known fact that many DevRels are more focussed on personal branding causing undue tension between employers and DevRels.

This blog is about personal branding - specifically, why personal branding for DevRels, how much is too much, and how do we balance individual advocacy with company goals?

Developer relations occupies a unique position at the intersection of engineering, product marketing, and community building. A DevRel always has more things on his/her head than the Job Description mandates. 

What is this Personal Branding? 

Personal Brand is something a Developer Relations professional accumulates over a period of time. Kind of an influence over a topic, or having a say over a category he/she advocates.
